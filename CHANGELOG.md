# Changelog

## [0.2.0] - 2026-04-07

### Added
- Drowsy eyelids animation during sleep

### Changed
- Mini-game polish: Tokyo city growth, football urgency effects, team colors

### Fixed
- Alignment for tears and cover-eyes sprites
- Scoreboard team color dot
- Removed unintended squish on mini-action and sleep transitions

## [0.1.0] - Initial release

- Pixel-art desktop companion for macOS (Apple Silicon)
- Wanders around and sits on window title bars
- Reacts to what you're doing (coding, browsing, idle)
- Plays with toys, runs mini-actions (football match, basketball, city growth)
- Grab and throw with your mouse
- Per-pet personalities and signature actions
- On-device LLM chat via Apple Foundation Models (macOS 26+)
- Auto-update with SHA256 + Ed25519 signature verification
